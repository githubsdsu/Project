About us



    We are final year engineering graduate students's from 
    D. Y. Patil's Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology, Nerul,
     Navi Mumbai, with a Specialisation in the field of 
     Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.
    This web Application is our final year engineering project created 
    with an aim of utilising the latest present-day Technology to 
    screen basic facial skin problems at the home itself.
    In the future it will also recommend skin care products to the user.
